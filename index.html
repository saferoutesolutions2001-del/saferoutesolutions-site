Privacy Policy - JRM Utility

Effective Date: 2026

JRM Utility is developed by Safe Route Solutions.

This application does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.

The application uses the device camera strictly for scanning QR codes required to retrieve Journey Risk Management (JRM) reports.

The application may download and temporarily store PDF documents on the user's device for offline access. These files remain within the application's local storage and are not shared externally.

No personal data, location data, analytics data, or tracking information is collected.

All document retrieval is performed via secure cloud storage connections.
